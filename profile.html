As of June 2020, the year of the pandemic, I made a bold yet significant decision to enrol onto the Code Institute's course to gain a Diploma in Software Development by upskilling in becoming a Full Stack Website Developer!

I graduated in 2010 which included undertaking a work placement year with Buoyant Upholstery Limited in 2007-08 whereby I developed the foundations into their new website from ground up on the basis of education & voluntary experience.

My work ethic is that I view a challenge like riding a road bicycle, clipped to the pedals and you're faced with a hill, whether small, medium or large; there's no turning back. The only way to get to the top is to roll up the sleeves, put every effort in, persevere and ride on no matter what or how hard that hill is to ascend in climbing up, the reward is at either the top or descending downhill.

Outside of utilising my technical skills in my spare time, I love to explore by being adventurous through cycling, hiking taking in the wonders of nature adventurous and enjoying the fun of snowboarding. I also love to practise the french vocabulary in my spare time, travel and visit museums.
